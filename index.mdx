## Welcome to Frata Robotics

Frata Robotics connects agricultural technology innovators with dependable logistics solutions.  
Located in Othello, Washington, the company provides secure storage, fulfillment, and field support for robotics, drone, and IoT manufacturers serving the United States agricultural market.
