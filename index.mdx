## Welcome to Frata Robotics

Frata Robotics bridges the gap between **agricultural innovation** and **real-world logistics**.  
From our base in **Othello, Washington**, we provide secure storage, fulfillment, and field support for robotics, drones, and IoT manufacturers serving the U.S. agricultural market.
